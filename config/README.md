# Career profile

`config/profile.md` is the private professional context your assistant builds
during onboarding. It captures the shape of your career, not just resume facts:

- career arc and throughline
- work that energizes you
- strengths and supporting evidence
- target directions and decision criteria
- practical constraints
- writing voice and phrases to avoid
- open questions worth revisiting

The tracked `profile.example.md` is a blank public schema. It is never a source
of facts about you. The onboarding interview proposes a separate
`config/profile.md`, shows it for approval, and saves it only after the privacy
confirmation in `AGENTS.md`.

The real profile is ignored by Git by default. Keep it on your computer or
follow the deliberate private-backup guidance in `docs/privacy.md`. Never place
real personal details in `profile.example.md`.

Career OS is a living system. When your interests, goals, constraints, or voice
change, ask your assistant to interview you about the change and show the exact
profile update before saving it.
