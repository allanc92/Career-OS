## [STORY-001] Helped customers become independent sooner

- **Pipeline state:** Filed
- **Availability:** Active
- **One-line summary:** Jordan found where customers stalled, redesigned the learning sequence, and reduced median time to first independent task from 18 days to 12 days.
- **Primary sources:** ACH-20260410-001; master resume

### Situation

Customers completed a fictional onboarding program but still needed repeated
facilitator help before performing the first core task independently.

### Task

Jordan was responsible for improving the learning program without adding
facilitator hours.

### Action

Jordan reviewed support themes and facilitator observations, mapped the steps
where learners stalled, rewrote the practice sequence, added a checkpoint, and
coordinated the revised rollout with facilitators.

### Result

Across 60 fictional learners, median time to first independent task decreased
from 18 days to 12 days. Facilitators also moved to one consistent workflow.
